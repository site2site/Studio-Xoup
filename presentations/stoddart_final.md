## Architectural API = Platform for Communicating Spaces


### Historically, architecture has defined the notion of place â€” fixing culture, people, and history to discrete locations on the planet. However, we are in transition, exchanging the stability and clarity of boundaries for an increase in connectivity. The Architectural API facilitates this exchange, a common language for augmenting the physical objects that already populate our environments to both collect and recieve data and to serve as conduits between physically separated spaces.
